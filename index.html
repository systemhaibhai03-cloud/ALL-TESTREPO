ℹ️ Accounting System — How It Works

1

Sales: Revenue bills yahan sales vouchers hain. Har sale me Project Manager assign karo.

2

Receipts: Party se paisa aaye to Receipt voucher banao — outstanding apne aap kam ho jaayega (FIFO bill-by-bill).

3

Purchases / Expenses / Payments: Manual entry — saari P&L me reflect hoti hai.

4

Stock: Har month ki opening stock manual daalo; closing stock month-end pe auto (weighted avg cost) nikal aata hai.

5

Outstanding: Project Manager wise receivables, bill-by-bill. Ledger me party-wise full statement.
